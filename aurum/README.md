# Aurum

[Aurum](https://pandasaurusgames.com/products/aurum?srsltid=AfmBOopp3pCJCuvUktg2cKYfp94ON0I2xM0-kwQN0liZk0g6RdOSOeak) ([boardgame geek](https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/383459/aurum)) is a must-not follow trick taking game. I had so much fun with it I wanted to implement it online. You should buy it before playing, but this is a way for me to play the copy I already own with my friends online. 

You can play this on [playingcards.io](playingcards.io) using the [aurum-4-players.pcio](./aurum-4-players.pcio) file in this repository (just import it into your custom room). This repository also contains the pictures and resources I used to make the game in playingcards.io. 

Disclaimers: This is not my property and I don't make money off of it, it's just a way for me to play the game I already own with friends online, since there is no way to do so currently. I made all the art myself using [Affinity Studio](https://www.affinity.studio/). Please support the creators by purchasing the official physical game.

## How to play

Create a custom room on [playingcards.io](playingcards.io) and import the [aurum-4-players.pcio](./aurum-4-players.pcio) file into your room. Then four players can log in and play. 

### Round Setup

- Place the round golden "Start Player" token next to the start player for the round
- Cycle the turn button to the start player's number
- Click **Deal Gold Cards** to set up the gold cards
- Click **Deal Base Metal Cards** to deal 25 cards per player and 2 extra cards (these two extra cards are not used and played face up beside the gold cards for all to see)
- Click the **Reset Points and Tricks** to reset the "Team # Tricks" and "Team # Points" for each team (this does not reset the "Team # Wins" counter, so make sure those are 0 when starting new game).
- Sort your cards in your hand and flip your bid card face down before placing it in the public play area
- Once all players have selected their bid cards
    - simultaneously reveal them
    - place the highest value bid card for the team in the appropriate holder (*Team # Bid*)
    - place the unused bid cards into the *Base Metal Cards* holder out of the way
- Play proceeds starting with the round start player
- Play your lead card into the *Trick* holder in the center of the board and click the turn button to cycle to the next player
- Once all 4 cards are played in the trick
    - The player who played the lowest value non-gold card takes the matching gold card from the supply (if available)
    - remove any gold cards that were played and return them to the supply.
    - The team who won the trick clicks the appropriate **Team # Take** trick button (team 1 should click the leftmost button, team 2 should click the rightmost button). 
        - The trick cards will automatically be moved to the appropriate holder, and the appropriate counter will be incremented
        - If you accidentally click the wrong button, you can manually adjust the trick counters and manually move the trick cards.
    - Move the golden "Start Player" token next to the new start player
    - Cycle the turn button to the new start player's number
- Once the round ends, tally your team's points using the provided counter, determine the winner of the round, and increment the appropriate "Win" counter
- If the game was not yet won, start a new round.
